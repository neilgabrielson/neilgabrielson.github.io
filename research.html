Pattern Formation PDEs for Biological Applications

I recently completed this research project as part of a Math REU at Northeastern University. I am working with professor Calina Copos, graduate mentor Tanishq Bhatia, and undergraduates Kaan Volkan and Naiwen Wang. We looked into various systems of partial differential equations (PDEs) that have been proposed to model protein interaction in the cell cortex.

Here is a description of our project and here is our final paper. Below, you can see cool animations of models from Michaud et al. (left) and Mori et al. (right) that I made in MATLAB. The code to make them, along with other models, annotations, and sources, can be found on our GitHub repository.
